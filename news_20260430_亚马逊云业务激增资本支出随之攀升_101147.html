亚马逊云业务激增，资本支出随之攀升

日期: 2026-04-30 10:11 | 查看原文 →
亚马逊云服务 (AWS) 受 AI 繁荣推动，净销售额同比增长 28%，达到 376 亿美元。首席执行官 Andy Jassy 将 AWS 的成功归功于为 AI 行业提供计算资源。然而，亚马逊也在投入巨额资金建设基础设施。Jassy 表示，资本支出增长在短期内将继续，因为 AWS 必须在盈利前提前投入资金购买土地、电力、建筑、芯片和服务器。由于资本支出增长显著超过收入增长，其自由现金流受到挑战，过去 12 个月的自由现金 flow 降至 12 亿美元，远低于 2025 年第一季度的 259 亿美元。

Original English Version

Amazon Web Services, buoyed by its role in fueling the AI boom, saw its net sales increase 28% year-over-year, climbing to $37.6 billion. Jassy attributed AWS' success to its role in providing compute to the AI industry. However, Amazon is also sinking increasingly large gobs of capital into building out the infrastructure. Jassy said that capital expenditure growth would continue in the near term. Amazon's free cash flow decreased to $1.2 billion for the trailing twelve months, driven primarily by a year-over-year increase of $59.3 billion in purchases of property and equipment.
